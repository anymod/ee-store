Frequently Asked Questions

What can I sell?

You can sell anything you see in the trade catalog. We are constantly adding new items, and we'd love to hear any suggestions you have.

Where should I sell?

You should sell wherever you think people will want to buy the product. That may be on your blog, with a mailing list, via social media, to your friends, or by purchasing advertising. Be creative, and feel free to experiment with pricing and different channels to see what works.

How do I get paid?

Money is deposited into your PayPal account 45 days after you make a sale. You must wait this amount of time to ensure that the customer has not initiated a return within the product's 30-day return window.

For now, payment is offered through PayPal only, so you will need a PayPal account to participate. We will be adding other payment methods soon, and we welcome your input on what you'd like to see.

Do I have to pay anything?

No. As a seller, you simply sell and get paid for it. However, if a product you sold is returned by the customer, money from that sale will not be paid into your account.

Who takes payment?

When the customer purchases a product, eeosk takes full payment. If the customer keeps the product, then eeosk distributes the seller's earnings to the seller and the supplier's earnings to the supplier.

Do my links ever expire?

As long as the product is available for purchase, your links will not expire. If the product is sold out or removed, your link will still work but customers will not be able to purchase the product.
