Returns Policy 101

  • Customers may return a product up to 14 days after it has been received.
  • Return the items in their original packaging.
  • Returns will only be accepted if the entire original item is included in the return.
  • Items that have been used or washed cannot be returned.
  • The customer will pay the return shipping costs.
  • If you receive damaged merchandise or there is a shipping or warehouse error, then the cost of shipping will be reimbursed by us as a credit to your account.
  • If a package is lost, stolen, refused, undeliverable, or returned to sender, a credit cannot be issued until the package is received back in our supplier's warehouse.
  • At the time of return please indicate whether you prefer a refund, exchange, or replacement product, and we will work to make that happen for you.

We will provide a full refund for items that arrive back to our warehouse in their original packaging and condition. Shipping cost is non-refundable, unless we made an error in the order.
